## Age Restrictions & COPPA Compliance

### Age Requirement

- **Minimum age:** 12+ years  
- **Statement:** “You must be at least 12 years old to use the App.”

### Account Creation

- Users confirm they meet the minimum age during registration.
- If we learn that an account belongs to a child under 12, we:
  - Disable the account.
  - Remove associated personal data where legally required.
  - Notify the parent/guardian where contact information is available.

## Child Safety Features

### Content Guidelines

Taatom prohibits:

- Sexual, exploitative, or suggestive content involving minors.
- Bullying, harassment, or hate against children.
- Dangerous challenges or content encouraging self‑harm or risky behaviour.
- Sharing of private information about a child (e.g. exact address, school name, phone number).

Violations may result in:

- Immediate content removal.
- Temporary or permanent account suspension.
- Reporting to authorities where legally required.

### Reporting & Moderation

- Users can **report posts, comments, or accounts** for:
  - Child safety concerns
  - Harassment or bullying
  - Nudity or sexual content
  - Self‑harm or dangerous behaviour
  - Spam or impersonation
- Reports are reviewed by a moderation team.
- Confirmed violations are removed and logged for repeat‑offender detection.

### Blocking & Restrictions

- Any user can **block** another account.
- Blocking prevents:
  - Direct messages
  - Comments
  - Follows and certain interactions
- Blocked users are removed from followers/following lists where applicable.

## Location & Privacy Controls

- Location tagging on posts is **optional**.
- Users can:
  - Choose whether to include location when posting.
  - Make their account **private**, limiting visibility of posts to approved followers.
- For child safety:
  - We recommend that young users **do not** share precise home or school address.
  - Parents/guardians should review their child’s privacy settings regularly.

## Parent & Guardian Guidance

We encourage parents/guardians to:

- Review the **Privacy Policy** and **Terms of Service** to understand how data is handled.
- Help minors set a **private** account if appropriate.
- Discuss safe sharing practices:
  - Do not share personal contact details publicly.
  - Do not post photos or videos that reveal home/school location.
  - Be cautious when interacting with strangers online.

If you are a parent/guardian and would like us to review or remove an account, contact us with:

- The **username** or profile link.
- A **brief explanation** of your request.
- Proof of guardianship if required by local law.

Contact: **contact@taatom.com**

## How to Report Child Safety Concerns

If you see content involving a minor that you believe is unsafe, harmful, or inappropriate:

1. **Report the content in the app** using the built‑in report tools.
2. **Block the user** if necessary.
3. For urgent cases, email **contact@taatom.com** with:
   - Links (URLs) to the content.
   - The username of the account involved.
   - A short description of why the content is harmful.

We will review and respond as quickly as possible and escalate serious concerns to the appropriate authorities when required.
